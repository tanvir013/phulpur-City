About Phulpur Upazila

Phulpur is a sub-district (upazila) of Mymensingh District in Bangladesh. It has a population of approximately 200,000 and covers an area of about 200 square kilometers. The area is known for its agricultural productivity, with rice, jute, and sugarcane being major crops. Phulpur is also home to several educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and local markets.

Local attractions include the Brahmaputra River, historical mosques, and vibrant local festivals.
